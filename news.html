N & J's New Location

Due to overwhelming supoort we have received from the community we are excited to announce we will be opening a second location in 2021 located in West Des Moines at Jordan Creek. We view this as a great opportunity to expand our reach and service to a broader community.

N & J's Awarded Best Burger at Iowa State Fair!

After entering our famous Monster Burger for the third year in a row, N&j's has been awarded the best burger award at the 2020 Iowa State Fair. Thank you to all of our loyal patrons for helping us improve and giving us the support necessary to acheive this incredible honor!
