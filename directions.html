Directions

We are located on North C Street in Indianola, IA.

If you are coming from Des Moines take I-35 South towards Kansas City until you reach the Highway 5 exit towards Norwalk. Then take Highway 5 to the Highway 65 exit at Indianola. Follow Highway 65 into town until you reach Chris Street Park, take a right and then an immediate left.

If you are having trouble finding us, do not hesitate to give us a call!
